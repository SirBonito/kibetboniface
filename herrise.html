Project Overview

HerRise is a group project created as part of the ALX Software Engineering program. The goal was to build a solution addressing a real-world problem—supporting and empowering women in society.

Key Features:

  • Community mentorship & support platform
  • Job and opportunity board tailored for women
  • Mental health resources
  • User-friendly interface based on wireframes

My Role:

I contributed to designing the wireframes, shaping the solution narrative, and helping prepare the pitch presentation. I also participated in research and ideation on the core features.
